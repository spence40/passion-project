Dress Code

Dress code varies depending on the style of dance you are taking. Below is a list of attire requirements for classes at Reflexions Dance Arts.

Please be dressed and ready 5 minutes before class begins. The instructor may excuse any dancers who come to class dressed improperly.

Preschool Dance/Ballet Combo: Leotard, footless tights, and ballet shoes. Hair secured away from face.

Dance Mix: Leotard, tights, ballet shoes, and tap shoes. Jazz shoes are optional. Hair secured away from face.

Ballet Technique Class: black leotard (no skirt attached), pink tights (footed or convertable) and split-sole ballet shoes. Hair in bun.

Leaps & Turns: Leotard, tightly fitted tank top, or sports bra. Tighly fitted shorts or leggings. Jazz shoes, pirouette shoes, or dance paws. Hair in bun.

Stretching & Conditioning: Tightly fitted athletic clothing, bare feet. Hair secured away from face.

Tap Technique: Leotard, tightly fitted tank top, or sports bra. Tightly fitted shorts of leggings. Tap shoes. Hair in bun.

Hip Hop: Comfortable athletic clothing, clean sneakers, hair secured away from face.

Jazz, Lyrical, Contemporary, Musical Theater: Leotard, tightly fitted tank top, or sports bra. Tightly fitted shorts of leggings. Jazz shoes, pirouette shoes, or dance paws. Hair in bun.

Acro/Tumble Classes: Leotard, tightly fitted tank top, or sports bra. Tightly fitted shorts or leggings. Bare feet, Hair secured away from face.

Adult Dance Classes: Comfortable athletic clothing that is not too baggy. Hair secured from face. Appropriate shoes for style of dance.

*In the Winter months, warm-ups are permitted while stretching. Warm-ups should be tightly fitted and easily removed. Examples are legwarmers, ballet sweaters, tight sweat pants, and fitted long sleeve shirts.
